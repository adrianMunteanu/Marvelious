Gabi is 28 years old and works as an Recruiter for a HR firm in Bucharest, which recruits qualified personnel for the IT industry in Iasi and Bucharest. She has worked in the job for more than two and a half years, and already knows her way around all the major social platform, and the ways they can be used for her job.

She feels very comfortable in her current position, and is eager to adapt and learn new ways and tools which can help her in her job. Even though she spends most of her time on a computer, writing emails and contacting people, she is not very interested in social media and IT in general. For her, the job is the only place where she interacts with a computer consistently.

Gabi's passion is watching movies, always seeing movies as soon as they are available in cinemas. With the current trend of Marvel movies, there are a lot of details which she cannot piece together, a lot of easter eggs she doesn't understand and a lot of characters she wants to know more about.

Being familiar social media, she tried to find more information there, but facebook groups and pages are not her thing, and se searches for a simple platform / application where she can find out the back-story of the characters from the movies she likes.

Gabi could never understand why some people love video games. Personally, she does not find them that interesting.
